Doyup Lee

Member of Technical Staff @ Runway

I am a member of technical staff at Runway, New York. I am the co-creator of Gen-3 Alpha, Frames, and Gen-4, which are the flagship products of Runway for media generation. Before that, I was a team lead at Kakao Brain and worked on visual content generation and representation learning. I received my Ph.D. from Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), South Korea, advised by Prof. Wook-Shin Han. I obtained my bachelor degree in Industrial Management Engineering from POSTECH.
