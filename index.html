Doyup Lee

I am the founder of a stealth startup focused on content creation and AI. Before that, I was a senior member of technical staff at Runway, New York, and a Foundational Contributor of Gen-3 Alpha, Frames, and Gen-4, which are the flagship products of Runway for media generation. I was a team lead at Kakao Brain and worked on visual content generation and representation learning. I received my Ph.D. from Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), South Korea, advised by Prof. Wook-Shin Han. I obtained my bachelor degree in Industrial Management Engineering from POSTECH.
